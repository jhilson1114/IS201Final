The LT6 Engine

An Engineering Marvel

To put it simply, the LT6 engine, the one that powers the new Z06, is a masterpiece. This engine was designed with the engine from the Ferrari 458 Italia in mind. It is a high reving naturally aspirated V8 that has a flat plane crank shaft. Oh and it sounds phenomenal!

This engine puts out 670 horsepower and 460 pound feet of torque. This power figure makes the LT6 the most powerful natrually aspirated V8 ever made. This is a huge accomplishment for Chevy!

The LT6 is a modern day technological marvel. It is a major reason why the Corvette Z06 is such a special car. We may be on the verge of the electrical revolution, but it is important to cherish such wonderful pieces of technology such as the LT6.
